**Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy applies to the ZeroTurn app for iOS mobile devices that was created as a free service.

**Information Collection and Use**

The following information is collected:

* Usernames provided by users
* Limited diagnostic information

Providing a username is optional and not required to play the game.

If provided, usernames are used solely to display rankings on our public leaderboard and may be visible to other users.

Usernames are stored using a third-party cloud provider (such as Amazon Web Services) to operate the leaderboard. This provider processes data only on our behalf and does not use it for its own purposes.

Usernames are retained until users request removal of their username.

Limited diagnostic information includes crash reports to help identify and fix issues in the app. This information may include device type, operating system version, and technical details about app performance. This data is used solely for debugging and improving the app and is not used to identify users.
