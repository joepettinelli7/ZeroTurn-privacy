**Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy applies to the ZeroTurn app for iOS mobile devices that was created by Joseph Pettinelli as a free service.

**Information Collection and Use**

The only information collected by this app is usernames provided by users. Providing a username is optional and not required to play the game.

If provided, usernames are used solely to display rankings on our public leaderboard and may be visible to other users.

Usernames are stored using a third-party cloud provider (such as Amazon Web Services) to operate the leaderboard. This provider processes data only on our behalf and does not use it for its own purposes.

Usernames are retained until users request removal of their username.

**Contact**

If you have any questions or requests regarding this privacy policy, you may contact joepettinelli@icloud.com.
